About Black Tiger Gaming Zone

Black Tiger Gaming Zone is Chitradurga's premier destination for an immersive and social gaming experience. We cater to teenagers and adults who are passionate about video games and want to connect with friends and family through shared gaming experiences. We provide a comfortable and exciting environment to enjoy the latest AAA titles.

Location and Overview:

Established in 2024, Black Tiger Gaming Zone is located in Ananth Nagar 1st Stage, Manipal, Udupi, and has quickly become the top choice for gamers in the area. We offer a state-of-the-art facility with high-end gaming equipment and a wide selection of popular games. Our mission is to provide a fun, safe, and social space for gamers of all skill levels to come together and enjoy the thrill of competitive and cooperative play.

Games and Services Offered:

Black Tiger Gaming Zone offers a wide range of AAA titles and gaming experiences, including:

  • Console Gaming: PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X consoles with a variety of controllers.
  • Racing Simulators: Immersive racing setups with realistic controls and feedback.
  • VR Gaming: Experience the latest in virtual reality gaming with cutting-edge VR headsets.
  • Arcade Games: Classic arcade games.

Our services include:

  • Group gaming sessions and events
  • Birthday parties and private events
  • Competitive tournaments with prizes
  • High-speed internet access
  • Comfortable seating and lounge areas
  • Snacks and beverages
